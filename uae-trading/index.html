Verdict

Buy Candidate
Strongest shortlist: trend, momentum, liquidity, volume, and risk filters are broadly aligned.
Setup Forming
Positive structure is developing, but the price, volume, or risk/reward still needs confirmation.
Worth Studying
Interesting enough for manual review, but not yet clean enough to treat as an actionable setup.
Watch
Mixed or neutral reading; keep it on the radar until trend or volume improves.
Sell Pressure
Weak tape or bearish trend pressure; useful mainly for exits or avoiding new buys.
Avoid / Ignore
Failed important filters, has poor data, or is too weak/illiquid for the current model.

Decision

Buy
Model sees a valid setup near the suggested buy zone with acceptable liquidity and risk/reward.
Hold
You marked it as bought, and the position has not hit an exit trigger yet.
Sell
A held position hit a stop, trailing stop, target/weakness rule, or time-based exit condition.
Watch
Quality is acceptable, but price location or risk/reward is not good enough to buy now.
Skip
No current action. Usually caused by weak setup quality, low liquidity, or poor risk/reward.

Setup

Trend Pullback
Price is in an uptrend and pulling back toward a support/EMA area instead of chasing strength.
Breakout
Price is pushing through recent resistance with momentum and volume support.
Mean Reversion
Price is extended below recent averages and may rebound, but this is usually lower conviction.
Exit Weakness
Structure is deteriorating; this matters most if the stock is already in your portfolio.
Unqualified
The stock does not currently match one of the model's actionable technical patterns.

Tier C
Too thin or inactive for this model. Usually skipped unless liquidity improves.
