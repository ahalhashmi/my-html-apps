Verdict

Buy Candidate
Strongest shortlist: trend, momentum, liquidity, volume, and risk filters are broadly aligned.
Setup Forming
Positive structure is developing, but the price, volume, or risk/reward still needs confirmation.
Worth Studying
Interesting enough for manual review, but not yet clean enough to treat as an actionable setup.
Watch
Mixed or neutral reading; keep it on the radar until trend or volume improves.
Sell Pressure
Weak tape or bearish trend pressure; useful mainly for exits or avoiding new buys.
Avoid / Ignore
Failed important filters, has poor data, or is too weak/illiquid for the current model.

Decision

Buy
Model sees a valid setup near the suggested buy zone with acceptable liquidity and risk/reward.
Hold
You marked it as bought, and the position has not hit an exit trigger yet.
Sell
A held position hit a stop, trailing stop, target/weakness rule, or time-based exit condition.
Watch
Quality is acceptable, but price location or risk/reward is not good enough to buy now.
Skip
No current action. Usually caused by weak setup quality, low liquidity, or poor risk/reward.

Setup

Trend Pullback
Price is in an uptrend and pulling back toward a support/EMA area instead of chasing strength.
Breakout
Price is pushing through recent resistance with momentum and volume support.
Continuation
Uptrend is intact and momentum remains supportive, but entry still depends on price location.
Reversal
Range/base support is holding after weakness. Lower conviction until confirmation improves.
Exit Weakness
Structure is deteriorating; this matters most if the stock is already in your portfolio.
Unqualified
The stock does not currently match one of the model's actionable technical patterns.

Grade

Grade A
Best technical stack: valid setup, good location, strong confluence, acceptable liquidity, and healthy risk/reward.
Grade B
Tradable watchlist quality, but one part of the stack still needs improvement.
Grade C
Interesting but not actionable. Usually needs a better entry, stronger zone, or more confirmation.
Grade D
Skipped by the model because setup, liquidity, regime, or risk/reward is too weak.

Structure

Regime
Classifies the chart as uptrend, downtrend, range, accumulation base, distribution, or mixed.
Location
Shows whether price is near demand/support, near supply/resistance, or stuck in the middle.
Zone
Scores nearby support/resistance from 0-14 using touches, recency, reaction, volume, and confluence.
Confluence
Scores how many independent factors agree, including zone quality, trend, averages, volume, and momentum.

Tier

Tier A
Best liquidity bucket. The model allows buy decisions only when other filters also pass.
Tier B
Tradable but thinner. The model can watch/study it, but it is more conservative on buys.
Tier C
Too thin or inactive for this model. Usually skipped unless liquidity improves.

Buy zone, stop, trailing stop, and targets are rounded to UAE market tick sizes. Treat them as decision-support levels, not automatic orders.
