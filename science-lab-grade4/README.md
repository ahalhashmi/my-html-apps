# Grade 4 Science Lab

Interactive Grade 4 final revision site built from the science revision sheet.

Topics covered:

- Magnet attraction and repulsion
- Broken magnets forming new north and south poles
- Magnetic and non-magnetic objects
- Electromagnet parts and strength
- Conductors, insulators, and electric flow
- Series and parallel circuits
- Transparent, translucent, opaque, reflection, and refraction

Quiz results are saved in the same browser with a shareable result link for parent review.
