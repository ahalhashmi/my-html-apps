# Historical World Cup data

The historical archive in `history.json` is derived from the Fjelstul World Cup Database.

Modifications: the source tables are filtered to the 22 men's tournaments from 1930 through 2022, joined into one compact JSON file, and adapted to the website's match, group, knockout, and scorer views. Scorer totals exclude own goals. Historical team flags are linked using country codes where a suitable flag is available.
